Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to PantryBloom. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you as to how we look after your personal data when you visit our application and tell you about your privacy rights and how the law protects you.

2. The Data We Collect

We collect, use, store and transfer different kinds of personal data about you which we have grouped together as follows:

  • Identity Data: Includes first name, last name, and profile identifier via Google Authentication.
  • Contact Data: Includes your email address.
  • Technical Data: Includes your device type, operating system, and camera access logs for barcode scanning.
  • Usage Data: Includes information about how you use our app, scanned item history, and pantry health scores.

3. How Your Data is Collected

We use different methods to collect data from and about you including direct interactions via Google Sign-In and automated technologies during app usage.

4. Data Security

We use Google Firebase for secure data storage and authentication. Your data is encrypted and protected by Google's industry-leading security infrastructure. We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties.

5. Children's Privacy

PantryBloom does not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13. If you believe we have such data, please contact us immediately.

6. Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions, please contact us at: support@pantrybloom.app

Refund Policy

PantryBloom is a digital utility designed to enhance your lifestyle and pantry health. As our app provides immediate access to digital tools and pantry intelligence, we generally do not offer refunds once a premium feature is accessed.

Any subscriptions or in-app purchases are managed directly through the Google Play Store and are subject to their standard refund terms.

About Us

PantryBloom was born from a simple vision: to transform the mundane task of pantry management into a curated, boutique experience. We believe that a healthy life starts with a healthy pantry.

We combine high-fidelity design with smart scanning technology to help you eliminate junk, track expiries, and discover the "Gems" in your kitchen through elegant, effortless technology.
